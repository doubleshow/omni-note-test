Translations

ON is currently translated in:

Arabic, Asturian, Catalan, Chinese Simplified, Czech, Dutch, English, French, Galician, German, Greek, Hebrew, Hindi, Indonesian, Italian, Khmer, Korean, Lao, Portuguese, Portuguese, Brazilian, Russian, Slovak, Slovenian, Spanish, Swedish, Turkish, Ukrainian

The translation project itself is opened to everyone wants to help, join the community of (great) translators on Crowdin!
